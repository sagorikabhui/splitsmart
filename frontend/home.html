FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SplitSmart!

Is SplitSmart completely free?
Yes! SplitSmart is completely free to use with no hidden charges. All core features including AI receipt scanner, UPI payments, and analytics are available for free!
How does the AI Receipt Scanner work?
Simply upload a photo of any bill or receipt. Our AI powered by Google Gemini automatically extracts the title, amount, and category — no manual entry needed!
How does UPI payment work?
When you need to settle up, click the UPI button next to the balance. It will open your UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) with the amount and recipient already filled in. Just confirm and pay!
What is Debt Simplification?
Debt Simplification is a smart algorithm that minimizes the number of transactions needed to settle all debts in a group. Instead of multiple payments back and forth, it calculates the most efficient way for everyone to settle up!
Is my data safe and private?
Yes! SplitSmart uses token based authentication to keep your account secure. Your data is never shared with third parties. You can delete your account anytime from Settings!
Can I use SplitSmart for international trips?
Yes! You can track expenses in any currency. Multi currency support with live exchange rates is coming soon in our Premium plan!
